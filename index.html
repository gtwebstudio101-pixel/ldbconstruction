About LDB

The Leader in
Super-Prime
Construction

LDB Construction was founded by Liam Birch in 2007. We are deeply committed to the business of building — working locally and internationally to construct the highest quality homes.

Our direct team, led by Managing Director Heath Adamson, offers a highly customisable service, facilitating in-depth discussions with global clients and their design teams to fully realise the goals of each project.

The company is owner-run, developed ethically and responsibly from the ground up without external investment. With our direct team of highly skilled in-house tradesmen, LDB has a significant edge over others who rely on casual labour.

Our Values

Principles That
Define Us

01

Workplace Wellbeing

The mental and physical wellbeing of our staff is an unyielding priority. We provide access to training and education, encouraging our staff to further their professional development through the Chartered Institution of Building.

02

Environmental Responsibility

LDB Construction takes continuous, practical steps towards our goal of becoming London's greenest super-prime residential contractor.

  • 1,000 new trees planted every year via Ecologi
  • 500,000 plastic rubble bags saved annually with reusable alternatives
  • 75% of site operatives cycle to work via LDB's Bike Scheme
  • 5% reclaimed materials target on all projects
  • Prioritising recycled content in all purchasing

Property Care

LDB's property care division channels our direct team of skilled in-house tradesmen to attend to small building works or alterations, continuing our client relationships after project completion.
